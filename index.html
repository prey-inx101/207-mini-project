My Background


My background is a blend of discipline and curiosity. I am the child of a retired Army (MSG) who served as a combat radio operator. My mother, who passed away in 2024, was a high school History Teacher who transitioned into education after a career in the financial sector. Their diverse professional paths provided me with a foundation built on clear communication and a deep respect for both analytical and narrative learning.

Within my family, I grew up as the only daughter and the middle child. Being situated between two brothers, one an Electrical Communications Engineer, and another a Resident Chef - College Instructor, exposed me to different perspectives, between technical logic, and creative work.

🧸 Collecting

BTS Albums

Back in December 2014, I was just graduating and felt completely new to everything. I kinda felt helpless and unsure what to do next. That was when I started following BTS because I resonated with how they began at the very bottom. Even today, their persistence inspires me to keep working hard as I try to get myself back on track.

🎀 Hello Kitty

My love for Hello Kitty also provides me a sense of comfort that started with a gift from my mom's coworker. I have always preferred her simple and cute design over any kind of dolls or any human-like toys since those actually kinda freak me out a bit.

☕ Friends × McDo

My Friends x McDo collection has been a way to divert my attention after my mom passed away in 2024. Watching the entire series became a way to cope with that loss and find some peace. Chandler Bing is my favourite character because his humor helped me through some of my darkest moments.
